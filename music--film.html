The Special Theory of Relativity

Fun Facts.

 

Yedhava Zindhagi and Dolphinium had overlapping shooting schedules. Dolphinium was only supposed to be an EP. While shooting for Yedhava Zindhagi in Tybee Island, Georgia, we encountered a kayaking spot amongst dolphins. The shoot was spot improvisation. The main logo of this website, the lighthouse, was also shot on this schedule.

Fun Facts.

 

The 'Meeku Meere Maaku Meme' director wanted an audition for the lyrics. The first set of lyrics were finalized on the spot. The song that was finalized was 'Uber oohallo' and it was the most peppy number on the album.

'By Unknown' was the first short film in the songwriting career. The director initially asked for a bit song. The team ended up commissioning the author for two songs, the scoring and co-music direction for the independent film.
